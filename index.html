Rescued 500+ stray animals in last 2 years!

Animal Care has helped and rescued more than 500 stray animals in past 2 years. We tried everything in our power so that each and everyone of them survived and lived a playful life.
Last 2 years weren't tough only for humans but also stray animals, they struggled very hard to get a single piece of bread. We tried our best that we help them in this tough time and we can proudly say that "Yes, we did!".
But we too need funds to function properly and cater the need of our furry friends and that's were we need your contribution and coopertion, Do donate and share the word.

Regularly fed more than 100+ stray animals during Covid

Covid was indeed a terrible phase of mankind but it severely affected animals too. Countless problems arise in their lives when our lives come to halt, stray dogs and cats usually feed on leftover from our society and house but during covid severe hygiene measures were taken and leftovers went to dumps getting not edible.
We here at Animal care made sure that none of them slept starving and did the best in our power. Breads, biscuits, milk, curry e.t.c for hundreds of animals were arranged and we did it for most of the covid phase.
It was tough and required lots of capital but we managed our finances and did the best.

Established a full fledged clinic!

Slowly and steadily we kept moving forward and with the help of constant support from you guys we managed to set-up our very own clinic.
We have a team of vets and rescuers available 24x7 waiting for your call for help, These people are here doing their work selflessly towards our furry mates and what they get in return is unconditional and eternal love from animals which is beyond any happiness!
We also have Mr.Help, our lovely chat-bot for your instant queries and quick-aid guides. You can use it by clicking the icon "Instant aid" and get answer for your minor queries.
We will be very grateful to you if you can help us run our process smoothly by donating funds and joining us as a volunteer.
